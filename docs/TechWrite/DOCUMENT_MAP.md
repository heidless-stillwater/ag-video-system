## 🌟 Top 5 Novice Features

These are the most critical features a new user must master to create their first documentary:

1.  **AI Research (Stillwater Global Intelligence)**:
    - *Why it matters*: It gather the factual foundation of your video.
    - *Novice benefit*: No manual research required; the system handles deep-crawling global databases.

2.  **Cinematic Scripting Subsystem**:
    - *Why it matters*: Converts raw data into a narrative.
    - *Novice benefit*: Automatically structures the documentary into logical scenes with voiceover text.

3.  **Multimodal Asset Synthesis**:
    - *Why it matters*: Generates unique, high-quality images and video clips.
    - *Novice benefit*: Eliminates the need for stock footage or manual image searching.

4.  **The Director’s Timeline**:
    - *Why it matters*: The central hub for project assembly.
    - *Novice benefit*: Simple drag-and-drop-style control over scene order and duration.

5.  **One-Click Publishing (Tactical Mastering)**:
    - *Why it matters*: Finalizes everything from audio to YouTube SEO.
    - *Novice benefit*: Handles the complex export and upload process automatically, ensuring the video is optimized for distribution.
